The World is Your Oracle is a compilation of divination techniques from around the world – both ancient and contemporary – designed for each individual’s unique situation, desire, and intuitive style. It gathers together dozens of methods, one-third of them visual, one-third auditory, and one-third kinesthetic. You can select a technique based on what appeals to you in the moment, one that dovetails with your intuitive strength (visual, auditory, or kinesthetic), or simply work your way through them for fun and greater self-knowledge.

Examples of divination techniques include;

  • Get guidance from the wild as you notice which what animal crosses your path
  • Prime yourself to dream about your question and awaken to a solution
  • Enter the wisdom of the spirit world by calling upon your guides and ancestors
  • Tap into your body’s hidden wisdom to get insight and answers.

LEARN which SENSE AWAKENS your INSIGHT

Are you visual, auditory, or kinesthetic?

Visual If you tend to be visually oriented, techniques like scrying, dream incubation, or balloon diagrams will help you develop your "second sight."
Auditory If hearing affects you most, listening to the "whisperings of intuition" – through birdsong, asking an ancestor for advice, or chanting - will help you tune into your inner, mystical knowledge.
Kinesthetic If touch nurtures you or you’re always in motion, connect with your "body wisdom" or "gut feelings" – through journaling, using a mudra, or sculpting clay.
